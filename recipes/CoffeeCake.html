Apple Coffee Cake

Description

A light and crumbly cake that is perfect for enjoying with a hot beverage

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 3/4 C of Sugar
  • 1/2 C Butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 C of plain yogurt or sour cream
  • 1/4 C of Milk
  • 1/2 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 tsp of nutmeg (or other spice of your choice)
  • 1 1/2 C of Flour
  • 1 tbsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

For the Filling

  • 5-6 Medium sized apples chopped into small pieces
  • 1/2 C sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp of lemon juice

For the topping

  • 1/4 C of Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp of Flour
  • 1 Tbsp of Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Begin by making the apple filling
  2. Coat the apple pieces in the lemon juice and then the salt, sugar, and cinnamon
  3. Heat them in a saucepan over medium heat and bring the pieces to a simmer. Continue to simmer until the apple pieces are soft and the sugar has carmelized. The filling should be a thick, spoonable texture like apple pie filling.
  4. Once done, put the apple filling into a jar or other heat resistant container and allow to cool while you begin making the cake.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  6. Sift the flour, your chosen spice, baking powder, nad salt together in a separate bowl and put it aside.
  7. Combine the softened butter and sugar together in a second bowl
  8. Add the eggs and stir until combined
  9. Add the yogurt, milk, and vanilla extract. You can add additional milk if the batter is too thick (difficult to stir or doughy)
  10. Divide the batter in half. Spread one half on the bottom of a greased backing pan
  11. On top of this batter layer, spread your cooled apple mixture
  12. Spread the second half of the batter on top
  13. Combine the topping ingredients in a bowl and mix until it appears homogenous in color.
  14. Sprinkle the topping on top of the cake, and then place in the oven
  15. Allow to bake for about 45 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  16. Allow to cool for 20-30 minutes before serving.
