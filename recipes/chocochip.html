Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A classic cookie recipe. Makes 2 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 C Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 C Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 C Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 16 oz of Chocolate Chips (or other minced chocolate)

Steps

  1. Soften the butter to room temperature and then cream it together with the sugar.
  2. Add the egg and the vanilla extract
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  4. Sift the flour, salt, and baking powder together in a second bowl
  5. Add the sifted dry ingredients to the butter/sugar mixture along with the chocolate chips. Stir until ingredients are just combined.
  6. Drop spoon sized balls of dough onto a greased or parchment lined baking sheet
  7. Bake for 15-18 minutes, or until cookies have turned golden brown on the bottom.
  8. Allow to cool for 20-30 minutes before removing from the pan. Cookies should slide off when removing with a spatula.
