3 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

A gluten free cookie recipe that is better than the original. Makes 2 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 C of Sugar
  • 1 C of Peanut Butter (natural preferred)
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream the sugar and peanut butter together. If using natural peanut butter be sure to either pour off the separated peanut oil, or ensure that it has been thoroughly mixed into the peanut butter before adding it to the sugar
  3. Add the egg and stir until combined
  4. Drop spoon sized balls of dough onto a greased or parchment lined baking sheet
  5. Bake until the cookies are golden brown around the edges
  6. Allow to cool for 20-30 minutes before enjoying
